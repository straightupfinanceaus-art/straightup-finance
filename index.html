FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What rate will I get?

Your rate depends on your financial situation and credit score. We compare multiple lenders to find you the absolute best rate available ‚Äî and we don't mark it up. Tell us what you're expecting and we'll let you know if it's achievable.

Can I pay off my loan early?

Yes! Most lenders allow early repayment. About 9 out of 10 lenders have an early exit fee of up to $800, but this decreases the longer you have the loan. For example, paying off a 7-year loan halfway through would typically mean around $400 in exit fees. If having no exit fees is important to you, let us know ‚Äî we can find lenders that offer this.

Will applying affect my credit score?

Getting a quote from us does NOT affect your credit file. We only perform a credit check when you decide to proceed with a formal application. So you can get a quote with complete peace of mind.

How long does the process take?

Approval typically takes 1-3 business days, sometimes same day depending on workload. Settlement takes another 1-4 days after approval. Our fastest ever? 4 hours from first contact to money in the account.

Do I need to know what I'm buying first?

No! We can provide pre-approval before you find your vehicle. This means you know exactly what you can spend before you start shopping, giving you stronger negotiating power with sellers.

What do I need to apply?

To get started, have ready: what you want to buy, purchase price and loan amount needed, where you're buying from, your living and work history for the past 3 years, your preferred loan term, and a rough idea of your assets and liabilities. We'll also need ID, income documents, and signed privacy consent forms.
