How Long Does Car Finance Take to Get Approved?

Published February 4, 2026 · 4 min read

When you need a car, you don't want to wait weeks for finance approval. So how long does the process actually take?

The Short Answer

Most car loan approvals take 24-48 hours. The entire process from application to settlement (when the money is transferred) typically takes about a week or less.

At Straight Up Finance, we've settled loans in as little as 4 hours when everything aligns. But that's not typical — it requires all your documents to be in order and a quick response from the lender.

What Affects Approval Time?

Your Documents

Having your documents ready is the biggest factor. Lenders typically need:

  • ID (driver's licence, passport)
  • Proof of income (payslips, tax returns if self-employed)
  • Bank statements (usually the last 3 months)
  • Details of the vehicle you're buying

If you have these ready to go, you'll move through the process much faster.

Your Application

Applications with straightforward circumstances (stable employment, good credit, standard vehicle) are processed faster. Complex situations (self-employed, unusual income, older vehicles) may require more assessment.

The Lender

Some lenders are faster than others. Banks can be slower due to their processes. Non-bank lenders and specialist car finance providers are often quicker.

The Typical Timeline

Car Finance Timeline

  1. Initial enquiry: You submit your details (5 minutes)
  2. Quote and application: We call you, discuss options, complete your application (15-30 minutes)
  3. Document submission: You send through your docs (10 minutes if prepared)
  4. Lender assessment: We submit to lenders and they assess (24-48 hours typical)
  5. Approval: You're approved and we present the offer (same day as assessment)
  6. Settlement: Contracts signed, funds transferred (1-2 business days)

How to Speed Things Up

  1. Have documents ready — Gather your ID, payslips, and bank statements before you start.
  2. Respond quickly — When we ask for information, the faster you reply, the faster we can move.
  3. Be upfront — Tell us everything relevant. Surprises during assessment slow things down.
  4. Get pre-approved — If you're still shopping for a car, get pre-approval first so you can move fast when you find one.

The Bottom Line

Car finance doesn't have to take forever. With the right broker and your documents ready, you can go from application to approved in 24-48 hours, and have funds settled within a week.
